Our Story

TechFix Pro was founded in 2019 with a simple mission: provide fast, reliable, and affordable repair services for everyday devices that people depend on.

What started as a small repair shop has grown into a trusted service center, thanks to our commitment to quality repairs, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer service.

Today, we’re proud to be certified by major manufacturers and trusted by hundreds of customers who know their devices are in expert hands.
